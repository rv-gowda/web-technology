Sudeep Sanjeev

Sudeep Sanjeev (born 2 September 1971),[3][4] also known as Sudeepa, is an Indian actor, director, producer, screenwriter, singer and television presenter, who primarily works in Kannada cinema. He has also worked in Hindi, Telugu and Tamil films. He is one of the highest paid actors of Kannada films and also featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list since 2013.[5] He has received several awards including four Filmfare awards, two Karnataka State Film Awards and one Nandi Awardand he is populary known as Abhinaya Chakravarty.
